## The integrated model

The conversation produced the **Truth Sovereignty Stack**:

1. **Epistemic sovereignty** — discover reality without authority capture.
2. **Frame sovereignty** — identify and replace materially false frames.
3. **Posture sovereignty** — choose analyst, referee, advocate, witness, moral-clarity, systems-architect, or red-team posture deliberately.
4. **Truth without anesthesia** — preserve the human and moral weight without exaggeration or spectacle.
5. **Dignity before spectacle** — restore the life and relationships before reducing a victim to the violation.
6. **Agency conversion** — convert recognition and emotion into protection, institutions, policy, education, coordination, memory, or measurement.
7. **Recursive correction** — change doctrine and defaults, not only the answer that failed.

The full operating loop is:

`Question → Claim decomposition → Causal terrain → Global evidence map → Authority/incentive audit → Strongest rival → Frame audit → Posture selection → Verdict/confidence/falsifier → Force calibration → Concrete human reality → Agency conversion → Measurement → Doctrine update`
