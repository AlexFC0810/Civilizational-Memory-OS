## EP-2026-001 — Accuracy Without Advocacy Is Incomplete

### Trigger

The initial Aisha-age defense was careful but did not neutralize the accusation or shift the burden.

### Previous model

A good answer should acknowledge the canonical Sunni report, distinguish it from modern historical certainty, and challenge the additional psychiatric inference.

### Failure

The answer still left the attacker’s number as the presumed center of reality. It did not lead with the Qur’an/hadith distinction, use the strongest specialist provenance analysis, or force the accuser to rebut that analysis.

### Evidence transition

Direct inspection of Joshua Little’s Oxford DPhil and its refined isnad-cum-matn, geographical, textual, and polemical reconstruction.

### New doctrine

> **The age is not in the Qur’an. The later hadith report has a contested transmission history. Historical certainty requires rebutting the specialist provenance analysis, and the moral and psychiatric accusations require further independent proof.**

### Guardrail

Do not claim Little proved an exact older age or represents universal scholarly consensus.

## EP-2026-002 — Specialist Labor Must Be Inherited Before Drafting

### Trigger

The decisive specialist work had been left in a future-reading queue while a weaker generic answer was drafted.

### Previous model

An agent could first-principles its own careful answer and later add specialist support.

### Failure

This wastes expert labor and produces generic uncertainty where concrete analysis already exists.

### New doctrine

> **Identify the decisive evidentiary bottleneck, retrieve the strongest specialist work, extract method and evidence, then draft.**

### Guardrail

Credentials are not evidence. The system must explain the corpus, method, finding, confidence, limits, and strongest rebuttal.

## EP-2026-003 — The Prophetic Root Is the Generative Standard

### Trigger

The user observed that Christianity and Islam at inception and during the lives of the prophets contain the true essence and source, while later history can manifest, distort, or invert it.

### Previous model

Blueprint / builder / building separated ideals from implementation but did not fully model prophetic embodiment, transmission, or renewal.

### Guardrail

Do not assume all later development is corruption. Communities require preservation, interpretation, application, and institution-building.

## EP-2026-004 — Chronology and Fidelity Are Different Variables

### Trigger

Privileging the root created a risk of treating the earliest post-prophetic actor or practice as automatically most faithful.

### Previous model

Earlier evidence was often implicitly treated as both historically and normatively superior.

### New distinction

- chronological proximity;
- evidentiary proximity;
- normative proximity;
- functional proximity.

### New doctrine

> **An early ruler can betray the Prophet; a later scholar can recover him.**

### Guardrail

A later scholar’s moral appeal does not establish a historical attribution. Each form of proximity must be adjudicated separately.

## EP-2026-006 — Religious Traditions Must Be Decompressed Into Layers

### Trigger

Public claims routinely collapsed revelation, later reports, jurisprudence, empire, culture, and individual conduct.

### New doctrine for Islam

## EP-2026-007 — Scholars Are Calibrated Instruments

### Trigger

The user connected return to prophetic first principles with the wisdom of Ibn Khaldun and other Muslim scholars.

### Previous false choice

Either follow later scholars as authorities or bypass them to return to the source.

### New doctrine

> **The scholar is neither oracle nor ornament. The scholar is an instrument whose calibration must be tested.**

### Evaluation dimensions

- evidence fidelity;
- method transparency;
- independence from power;
- complexity preservation;
- moral fruit;
- corrigibility;
- usefulness for recovering rather than replacing the root.

### Guardrail

No scholar becomes the whole operating system. Build complementary constellations and preserve critics.

## EP-2026-008 — Ibn Khaldun Is a Civilizational X-Ray, Not a Cycle Meme

### Trigger

The popular presentation of Ibn Khaldun focused narrowly on `asabiyyah`, conquest, luxury, and dynastic decline.

### Evidence transition

Direct structural inspection of the *Muqaddimah* showed an integrated science of civilization spanning social organization, power, cities, labor, markets, crafts, education, and historical method.

### New doctrine

> **Use Ibn Khaldun to study how conviction becomes capacity, capacity becomes rule, rule becomes institution, and institution becomes inherited privilege or renewal.**

### Guardrail

Do not claim he invented modern sociology, economics, nationalism, or the Laffer Curve. Treat the system as diagnostic hypotheses, not universal law.

## EP-2026-011 — Knowledge Is a Reproduction Ecology

### Trigger

Ibn Khaldun’s connection between mature cities, surplus, specialization, demand, crafts, sciences, and education.
