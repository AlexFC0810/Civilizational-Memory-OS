# Contested Islam Claim-Family Registry

**Status:** Proposal / research intake registry  
**Date:** 2026-07-13  
**Write policy:** Suggestion branch only; no entry receives a verdict from being listed  
**Purpose:** Track recurring accusations, smears, difficult questions, and valid criticisms as claim families rather than isolated clips.

---

## 1. Why a registry

The public information environment repeats a relatively small number of claim families through thousands of personalities, videos, headlines, and incidents.

Researching every clip from scratch wastes attention and prevents knowledge from compounding.

The durable unit should be:

> **claim family → exact variants → source layers → evidence record → verdict → response forms → refresh policy**

The registry must not pre-judge every accusation as false merely because it is hostile.

> **Some entries will be false. Some misleading. Some disputed. Some offensive but factual. Some will expose genuine Muslim or Islamic historical failures. Registration is not acquittal or conviction.**

---

## 2. Prioritization model

Rank work by:

```text
Priority =
  harm if believed
× recurrence
× audience reach
× transfer value
× current timeliness
× probability that source closure changes understanding
÷ research and production cost
```

Also score:

- ease of source closure;
- risk of amplifying a fringe claim;
- availability of a load-bearing distinction;
- creator demand;
- connection to current transcript packets;
- whether the claim unlocks multiple downstream families.

The objective is not to answer the most insulting claim first.

It is to answer the claim whose clarification repairs the largest part of the public map.

```text

    ↓
ATOMIZE
    ↓
CLASSIFY SOURCE + CLAIM TYPE
    ↓
ASSESS HARM / RECURRENCE / REACH / TRANSFER / COST
    ↓

    ↓

    ↓

    ↓

    ↓
```
