## 4. Evidence court split

### Internal religious/traditional court

What is authoritative within the named tradition, school, or methodology?

### Modern historical-critical court

What can be dated, corroborated, or reconstructed historically, and to what confidence?

### Moral court

Which facts are relevant to moral judgment? Which contextual facts explain without excusing?

### Legal/clinical court

What elements must be proved before a legal or psychiatric label applies?

### Collective and modern-policy court

What, if anything, follows for modern adherents, law, policy, or institutional design?

Never silently transfer a verdict from one court into another.
