## Confidence level
Choose one:

- **High:** well-supported by strong sources and broad scholarly agreement.
- **Medium:** supported, but requires qualification or context.
- **Low:** plausible or interesting, but not ready for public use.
- **Disputed:** meaningful disagreement exists; present multiple views.
- **Do not publish yet:** needs more research.
