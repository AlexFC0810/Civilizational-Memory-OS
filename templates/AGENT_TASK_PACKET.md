## 9. Stop conditions

- the definition of done is met;
- a required source cannot be retrieved;

## 10. Escalation conditions

- a dependency is missing;
- repository routing is unclear;
- the task conflicts with another active packet;
- the output implies a different P0 constraint;
- the work should be split.

Escalate to the Coherence Sentinel when:

- source layers conflict;
- a public-strength boundary is unclear;
- a canonical contradiction is discovered;
- identity-wide blame or material harm risk appears;
- proposed supersession affects load-bearing canon.

Escalate to the founder when:

- public outreach or publication is proposed;
- a creator's representation or consent is implicated;
- major spending or an irreversible action is required;
- Steward and Sentinel cannot resolve a material dispute.
