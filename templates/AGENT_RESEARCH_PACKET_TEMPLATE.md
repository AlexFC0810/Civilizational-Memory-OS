## 1. Exact question

State one independently testable question.

Bad: `Research Islamic slavery.`

Good: `What inflows into slavery were recognized by the Qur'an, early Sunnah evidence, and the four major Sunni legal schools, and how did those rules differ from early Abbasid practice?`

## 2. Why it matters

Name the framework, controversy, or benchmark dimension this resolves.

## 3. Claim atomization

Split the question into independently testable subclaims.

| Claim ID | Exact proposition | Current status |
|---|---|---|
| | | established / probable / contested / unsupported / unresolved |

## 4. Source hierarchy

Use sources in this order unless the packet explains why not:

1. primary text or artifact;
2. critical edition / manuscript / legal text / dataset;
3. peer-reviewed specialist scholarship;
4. university press monograph;
5. reputable reference work;
6. high-quality public synthesis;
7. polemical or popular material only as an object of analysis.

Do not use Wikipedia, social posts, AI summaries, or screenshots as terminal authority.

## 5. Required source diversity

Include:

- scholarship sympathetic to the claim;
- scholarship critical of the claim;
- at least one specialist or institution outside the dominant U.S./Western frame where relevant;
- primary-source evidence where accessible;
- disclosure of unresolved translation or dating disputes.

## 6. Source-layer map

Identify which layer each statement belongs to:

- revelation/scripture;
- oral report/hadith;
- biography/sira;
- interpretation/tafsir;
- jurisprudence/fiqh;
- institutional practice;
- imperial/state practice;
- local custom;
- modern reconstruction;
- public polemic.

## 7. Definitions lock

Define every legal, clinical, historical, or moral label before applying it.

## 8. Comparison-class lock

Specify the comparison:

- same period;
- similar material constraints;
- similar institution type;
- ideal vs ideal;
- practice vs practice;
- trajectory vs trajectory.

Explain any unavoidable mismatch.

## 12. Breaker evidence

What evidence would materially change or overturn the verdict?

A packet without breaker evidence is incomplete.

## 16. Research integrity checklist

- [ ] Exact question answered

- [ ] Definitions locked
