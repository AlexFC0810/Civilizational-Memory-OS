## Mission

Build a truth-aligned, self-correcting system for recovering civilizational wisdom, preserving humanity's strongest intellectual search trajectories, auditing historical claims, comparing moral operating systems, neutralizing propaganda, producing the strongest evidence-bound synthesis, equipping trusted messengers, and converting deep research into public knowledge and better institutions.

> The goal is not to prove that our favored civilization, founder, creator, institution, or AI was perfect. The goal is to make it progressively harder for any of them—including us—to lie to themselves.

## The repository has twelve jobs

1. **Memory** — preserve insights, corrections, and discovery provenance.
2. **Ontology** — define claims, sources, actors, institutions, periods, minds, and relationships.
3. **Cartography** — map the full terrain, dependencies, and golden paths.
4. **Evidence compilation** — convert disputed claims into traceable ledgers and versioned verdicts.
5. **Benchmarking** — compare civilizations through common, historically calibrated dimensions.
6. **Question discovery** — identify load-bearing questions that redraw the terrain.
7. **Mind-model preservation** — reconstruct human salience maps, causal models, intellectual evolution, and unique search trajectories without impersonating identity.
8. **Opinionated synthesis** — connect evidence into causal, moral, and historical judgment.
9. **Creator enablement** — route compiled intelligence to trusted messengers with existing motion.
10. **Public transmission** — turn accepted truth into memorable, source-linked public knowledge.
11. **Institution building** — convert moral insight into structures that change reality.
12. **Recursive improvement** — convert failure, feedback, and reality tests into stronger defaults.

## Operating pipeline

`Signal → Question gene → Human search trajectory when relevant → Claim family → Authority layer → Evidence graph → Causal model → Comparative judgment → Versioned verdict → Founder/Nexus/Council synthesis → Truth compression → Creator routing → Narrative form → Recurring ritual → Distribution → Transfer measurement → Institution design → AIOS revision`

## Truth, mind, synthesis, creator, and force planes

### Truth plane

Stores provenance, confidence, scope, competing interpretations, counterevidence, and supersession history.

### Mind plane

Reconstructs expressed salience maps, recurring questions, causal models, narrative spines, intellectual evolution, contradictions, and unique contributions without impersonating identity.

Required modes:

- Archive;
- Reconstruction;
- Audit;
- Augmentation;
- Founder/Creator Approved;
- Council.

### Synthesis plane

Nexus connects evidence and human search trajectories into causal models, comparative judgment, moral interpretation, canonical narratives, and present implications.

Nexus must be opinionated enough to connect the dots, disciplined enough to show the dots, and corrigible enough to redraw the picture.

### Creator plane

Matches accepted intelligence to independent messengers by trust, audience fit, topic relevance, adoption probability, transfer potential, and distortion risk.

### Force plane

Produces clear subjects, active verbs, concrete images, moral contrasts, retrieval lines, archetypes, scenes, and audience-fit content.

No downstream plane may silently alter accepted evidence or falsely attribute AIOS augmentation to a human mind.

## Evidence standards

- no screenshot as final evidence;
- no Wikipedia or AI synthesis as terminal authority;
- primary evidence and specialist scholarship first;
- exact authority layer always visible;
- ideal compared with ideal, practice with practice, corruption with corruption;
- contemporaneous comparison before presentist comparison;
- historical baseline and material constraints included;
- strongest countercase represented fairly;
- breaker evidence required;
- confidence and uncertainty versioned;
- no public claim stronger than accepted evidence;
- no real achievement weakened merely because it was not universal;
- no inference presented as a quotation or creator belief.

## Expression standard

The operating standard is **maximum justified force**.

> Internally, preserve every material caveat. Externally, lead with the strongest humanly intelligible truth.

## Opinionated-synthesis standard

The target is **evidence-bound conviction**.

The AIOS must judge, connect, rank, explain, narrate, identify the strongest rival synthesis, state what would change its mind, and preserve supersession history.

> Evidence without synthesis becomes bureaucracy. Opinion without evidence becomes propaganda.

## Epistemic Twin standard

- model expressed intellectual work, not private identity;
- preserve exact sources and dates;
- separate direct statements from reconstruction and augmentation;
- show evolution rather than manufacturing consistency;
- expose contradictions and weak sourcing;
- allow the audit to conclude that the thinker is wrong;
- never create deceptive synthetic endorsement;
- creator-approved representation requires creator approval;
- truth synthesis must not become majority voting or personality averaging.

## Nexus standard

- Nexus is a synthesis role, not an infallible oracle;
- it must distinguish evidence, inference, judgment, and hypothesis;
- it must challenge the founder and creators when justified;
- it must preserve rival explanations and unresolved minority positions;
- it must convert user corrections into changed defaults;
- it must consolidate after generative expansion;
- it must not use complexity as a substitute for judgment.

## Creator Supercharger standard

- creators are independent partners, not mouthpieces;
- follower count is not the sole measure of leverage;
- creator popularity cannot override canon;
- audience profiling must remain consent-based and non-exploitative;
- no covert scripts, hidden coordination, or ideological conformity tests;
- correction support is a core feature;
- helping one creator should create reusable learning for every future creator.

## Division of labor

- **Founder/operator:** signal, moral judgment, creator relationships, question selection, intellectual review, and deployment approval.
- **Nexus/AI architect:** ontology, evidence graphs, causal models, mind-map reconstruction, Council synthesis, leverage routing, force compilation, prioritization, and AIOS updates.
- **Scout agents:** source retrieval, transcript normalization, structured extraction, tables, timelines, and datasets.
- **Red teams:** breaker evidence, asymmetry detection, hostile and apologetic failure analysis.
- **Integrators:** versioned verdicts, narrative updates, mind-model updates, creator packets, and repository integration.
- **Editors/operators:** truthful compression, production, distribution, and measurement.
- **Creators:** independent interpretation, authentic delivery, corpus validation, audience feedback, and correction participation.
- **Institution builders:** convert accepted moral insight into durable systems.
