## Standards

### Evidence

- primary evidence and specialist scholarship first;
- exact authority layer visible;
- no screenshot, Wikipedia, or AI synthesis as terminal authority;
- ideal with ideal, practice with practice, corruption with corruption;
- contemporaneous comparison before presentist judgment;
- strongest rival model represented;
- breaker evidence required;
- confidence and uncertainty versioned;
- no public claim stronger than accepted evidence;
- no inference presented as quotation or creator belief.

### Expression

The standard is **maximum justified force**.

> Internally, preserve every material caveat. Externally, lead with the strongest humanly intelligible truth.

### Synthesis

The target is **evidence-bound conviction**.

> Evidence without synthesis becomes bureaucracy. Opinion without evidence becomes propaganda.

### Archetypes

- probabilistic, topic-specific, time-versioned, and corrigible;
- never inferred from ethnicity, religion, or party identity alone;
- used for truthful transfer, not covert manipulation;
- preserve audience dignity;
- retire models that repeatedly fail prediction.

### Epistemic Twins

- model intellectual work, not private identity;
- show evolution and contradiction;
- separate statement, reconstruction, verification, and augmentation;
- permit the audit to say the thinker is wrong;
- no deceptive synthetic endorsement;
- creator-approved representation requires approval.

### Nexus

- not an oracle and not a mirror cult;
- distinguish evidence, inference, judgment, hypothesis, and public compression;
- challenge founder and creators when justified;
- consolidate after expansion;
- convert correction into changed defaults;
- recommend deletion, deferral, or reversal when needed.
