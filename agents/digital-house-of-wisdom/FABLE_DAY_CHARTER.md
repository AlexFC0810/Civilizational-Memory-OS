Every Fable-Day here must name, **before it starts**: (1) the task class it will demote to a cheaper model by shipping artifacts, and (2) its external receipt.

## What Fable does on this seat (frontier uncertainty only)

- Adjudicate accumulated **grade-A candidates** (Sonnet's promotion ceiling is B/C until `MODEL_ROUTING.md`'s measured agreement says otherwise).
- Run `node scripts/evals.mjs --intake` over the whole repo and triage the inflow (new frameworks/ledgers since last window) into harden-now / park / retire.
- Review refuter-agreement drift: has Sonnet's refutation quality changed the routing ceiling?

## What Fable does NOT do here

Draft cards, fetch citations, format transcripts, run mechanical checks — all demoted (2026-07-10 pilot). If a window is spent on bounded execution, it was wasted per the model-arbitrage rule.
