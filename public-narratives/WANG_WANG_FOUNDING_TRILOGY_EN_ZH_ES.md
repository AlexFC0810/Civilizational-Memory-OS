# II. THE VOICELESS COVENANT

## English

### The Voiceless Covenant

We affirm that a being’s inability to speak, vote, testify, own property, organize, or retaliate does not weaken its claim against cruelty.

It strengthens our duty of protection.

We reject the idea that ownership means unlimited permission.

We reject cruelty as entertainment, status, discipline, profit, or tradition.

We will teach children that mercy is strength, that animals experience fear and pain, and that filming abuse is not neutrality.

We will protect evidence without rewarding spectacle.

We will build laws, schools, platforms, markets, and communities that intervene before suffering becomes irreversible.

We will judge power by what it refuses to do to those entirely beneath it.

**The voiceless are not ownerless.**

---

## 简体中文 / Simplified Chinese

### 无声者公约

我们相信：一个生命无法说话、投票、作证、拥有财产、组织行动或反抗，并不会削弱它免受残酷对待的权利。

相反，这更加强了我们保护它的责任。

我们拒绝把“拥有”理解为不受限制的支配权。

我们拒绝以娱乐、炫耀、惩罚、利益或传统之名实施残忍行为。

我们要教导孩子：慈悲是一种力量；动物能够感受到恐惧和痛苦；拍摄虐待行为并不等于保持中立。

我们要保存证据，但绝不奖励或传播残酷的表演。

我们要建立能够在痛苦无法挽回之前及时介入的法律、学校、平台、市场和社区。

我们将用一个标准衡量权力：面对完全处于自己掌控之下的生命，它选择拒绝做什么。

**无法发声，不代表无人守护。**

# III. BE THE ONE WHO PROTECTS

## English

### For children and teenagers

When others begin hurting an animal:

1. **Do not join them.**
2. **Say stop if you can do so safely.**
3. **Move the animal away only if it is safe.**
4. **Get a trusted adult immediately.**
5. **Call emergency or animal-protection services when needed.**
6. **Record only what is necessary for evidence—never for entertainment.**
7. **Do not repost graphic cruelty. Give the evidence to someone who can act.**

You may be the only person in the group who refuses.

That does not make you weak.

**Be the one who protects.**

当别人开始伤害动物时：

3. **只有在安全的情况下，才把动物带离现场。**
4. **立即寻找值得信任的成年人帮助。**
5. **必要时联系警方、紧急服务或动物保护机构。**
6. **只有在确有必要时才为取证进行拍摄，绝不能为了娱乐。**
7. **不要转发残酷的画面。把证据交给能够采取行动的人。**

你可能是人群中唯一拒绝参与的人。

这并不代表你软弱。

**成为那个保护生命的人。**

## 4. Visual direction

### Emotional objective

Grief transformed into guardianship—not helplessness, gore, vengeance, or ethnic blame.

### Hero image

A dignified mother dog sheltering puppies in warm light. She should appear alive, protective, and morally present—not injured.

### Symbol system

- protective arc / shelter;
- small paw within open hands;
- candle used sparingly for memorial context;
- seedling representing protection becoming future culture;
- no crown unless deliberately explained; avoid accidental anthropomorphic hierarchy;
- no graphic weapons, fire, wounds, or reenactment.

### Design rule

Do not place three full languages in one dense poster as the primary social asset.

Create separate localized versions using one shared visual system:

- English;
- Simplified Chinese;
- Spanish.

This improves legibility, sharing, cultural adaptation, and quality control.

### Recommended launch formats

- 1080 × 1350 memorial post;
- 1080 × 1920 story/reel cover;
- 1080 × 1080 doctrine card;
- 6-slide carousel;
- printable classroom mini-poster;
- text-only accessible version.
