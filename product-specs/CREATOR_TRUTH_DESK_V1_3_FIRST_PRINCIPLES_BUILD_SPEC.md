# 1. Product Verdict

The current desk has crossed from styled briefing into a credible operating instrument.

Preserve:

- the Burden Engine: **Prove it · Connect it · Justify it**;
- Briefing / Live / Study modes;
- search and copy interactions;
- source-state labels;
- truth boundaries;
- compressed clip-safe answers;
- “Lines to retire”;
- Golden Age Futurism visual language;
- the source-layer astrolabe as a signature interaction;
- the advocate-not-referee orientation;
- correction and recursive-improvement loops.

The next version must not become a wholesale redesign. It is a structural correction and operational compression.

---

# 2. North-Star User Outcome

On a phone, during a hostile live exchange, the operator should be able to:

1. recognize the accusation;
2. retrieve the relevant seven-second response;
3. ask the correct burden question;
4. concede the true fragment without surrendering the inference;
5. return to the source standard;

in under five seconds and with one hand.

The audience should leave understanding one durable concept:

> **“Islam says” is not a source citation. Name the layer and trace it to the root.**

# 3. P0 — Required Before Outreach

## P0.1 Add the Source Constitution to the hero

The hero needs an explicit first-principles opening before the one-move burden line.

### Recommended hero doctrine

> **The Qur’an is revelation. The Sunnah is the Prophet’s lived model. Hadith are transmitted reports about that model. Fiqh is human jurisprudence. Governments and individuals are implementations. Before anyone says “Islam says,” quote it, name the layer, and trace it to the root.**

### Recommended short label

`THE SOURCE CONSTITUTION`

### Interaction

- show the 20-second version by default;
- include a copy button;
- expandable 60-second debate opening;
- hide the long version in Live mode;
- pin the seven-second anchor in Live mode:

> **Quote it · Name the layer · Trace it to the root**

## P0.2 Rebuild the astrolabe as a Prophetic Root map

Preserve the visual signature, but change the intellectual model.

### Inner ring — normative chain

1. Qur’an — revelation
2. Prophetic Sunnah — lived embodiment
3. Hadith and sira — transmitted evidence
4. Tafsir / theology / fiqh — interpretation

### Outer ring — implementation chain

5. scholarly and communal institution
6. state law / imperial practice
7. movement / inherited culture
8. individual action

### Audit ring

9. historical fruit

### Center

`TRACE THE CLAIM`

### Hover/tap content

Each node should answer:

- What is this layer?
- What authority can it legitimately claim?
- What can go wrong here?
- What proof is required before attributing it to Islam?

### Visual distinction

- revelation: brass;
- Prophetic embodiment: luminous teal/brass bridge;
- transmitted evidence: teal;
- interpretation: desaturated teal;
- institution/state: blue;
- movement/individual: grey-blue;
- historical fruit: outer dotted ring, not another source of revelation.

### Required disclaimer

> **This is an authority-and-transmission map, not a claim that the Qur’an is the only Islamic authority. Mainstream Sunni Islam treats authentic Prophetic Sunnah as binding; the map distinguishes the Sunnah from later reports used to reconstruct it.**

## P0.3 Migrate the hardened Aisha defense

Replace brittle language such as “a single late hadith chain.”

### Required public-safe core

> **“That age is not in the Qur’an. It comes through later hadith reports. Joshua Little’s Oxford DPhil collated the available versions and argues that they likely descend from a report associated with Hisham ibn Urwah’s Iraqi period rather than multiple independent eyewitness streams. That does not prove one exact older age—but it defeats the pretense that nine is equivalent to an uncontested contemporary record.”**

Then add:

> **“Even after proving the number, you still have to prove prepubescence, coercion, injury, and the psychological diagnosis attached to it. Those are separate claims.”**

### Required prohibited lines

- “There was only one chain.”
- “Joshua Little proved she was older.”
- “Joshua Little proved all hadith are unreliable.”
- “Aisha was definitely eighteen or nineteen.”
- “The canonical report does not exist.”

### Internal references

- `defense-dossiers/001_AISHA_AGE_EVIDENCE_AND_DEFENSE.md`
- `canon/SPECIALIST_HEAVY_LIFTING_PROTOCOL.md`

## P0.4 Rewrite the demographic / H-1B reversal

Remove any move that answers one collective suspicion by redirecting suspicion toward another ethnic or religious population.

### Retire

- Hindu / Chinese / Mexican demographic comparisons as counter-targets;
- “aim at the wrong door” framing;
- replacement rhetoric that accepts the opponent’s dehumanizing premise.

### Replace with

> **“Muslims are approximately one percent of the United States. You have not shown a demographic takeover, a legal mechanism for replacing the Constitution, or a named organization capable of doing it. You have described a fear, not demonstrated a threat.”**

### Reversal

> **“You reject collective guilt when it targets people you identify with. Keep the same standard when the minority is Muslim.”**

## P0.5 Simplify the manufactured-threat reversal

Preserve the valid structural point while removing unnecessary factual dependencies.

### Recommended version

> **“You spent years explaining how governments take a real threat, inflate it into an enemy class, and use that class to manufacture consent. Why are you now taking crimes committed by particular actors and turning two billion people into the enemy category?”**

### Source-close before reintroducing

- claims about who pioneered specific terrorism vocabulary;
- “seven countries” chains;
- PNAC-to-specific-Israel-goal claims;
- any statement that requires defending a complete geopolitical causal theory during a religious debate.

## P0.6 Replace vulnerable universal-killing citation use

Do not rely on Qur’an 5:32 as though its wording has no stated Children-of-Israel context.

Where the objective is universal prohibition of unlawful killing, prioritize a source-closed treatment of:

- `Q 6:151`;
- `Q 17:33`;
- `Q 25:68`.

Where the objective is rejection of collective guilt, preserve:

- `Q 6:164`;
- `Q 17:15`.

Qur’an 5:32 may remain with context and interpretive explanation, not as a context-free mic-drop.

## P0.7 Calibrate the “law of the land” claim

Retire:

> “Classical sharia tells Muslims to obey the law of the land.”

Replace with a sourced, qualified formulation:

> **“Muslim jurists generally treat covenants, contracts, and lawfully entered citizenship or residency commitments as binding, except where a person is compelled into wrongdoing.”**

Flag for specialist legal review before formal outreach.

# 4. P1 — Live Mode as a Combat Console

Live mode should feel materially different from Study mode.

## Persistent top bar

Display only:

> **ONE CLAIM · QUOTE IT · NAME THE LAYER · PROVE THE INFERENCE**

Secondary line:

> **Prove it · Connect it · Justify the remedy**

## Every live claim card

Show four fields only:

1. **7-second line**
2. **Burden question**
3. **True concession**
4. **Reversal**

Everything else remains collapsed.

## Emergency reset controls

Add copyable buttons:

### Flood reset

> “You made five new claims without resolving the first. Return to the original charge.”

### Source reset

> “That was a story about a person. Where is the Qur’anic command?”

### Layer reset

> “You moved from a hadith to an empire to me. Pick one layer.”

### Inference reset

> “Even if that fact is true, the conclusion still does not follow.”

### Remedy reset

> “Explain why that would justify collective guilt, censorship, deportation, or war.”

## Search behavior

- prioritize synonyms and opponent wording;
- search results should surface Live cards first;
- keyboard `/` and mobile search should remain;
- include aliases: Aisha / age / nine / child; sharia / law / constitution; jihad / holy war / terror; jizya / convert / tax / die.

# 5. P1 — New Claim Information Architecture

Study mode should support the full sixteen-field schema in the doctrine.

### Above the fold

- exact accusation;
- status badge;
- source layer;
- seven-second line;
- burden question;
- true concession;
- missing inference.

### Expanded evidence

- Qur’anic anchor or explicit silence;
- Prophetic embodiment;
- report provenance;
- interpretation state;
- institutional history;
- fruit test;
- source packet;
- prohibited overclaims;
- confidence state;
- correction history.

### Status vocabulary

Use a controlled set:

- `SOURCE-CLOSED`
- `STRONG`
- `PROVISIONAL`
- `DISPUTED`
- `SPECIALIST REVIEW`
- `OPERATOR-GATED`
- `RETIRED`

Do not use decorative confidence language that is not tied to evidence requirements.

# 6. P1 — Fruit Test Module

Add a reusable module titled:

> **HISTORICAL FRUIT — WHAT DID THE ROOT ACTUALLY PRODUCE?**

Its function is to test universal allegations with historical evidence.

## Structure

- **Universal accusation**
- **Historical counterevidence**
- **What the counterexample proves**
- **What it does not prove**
- **Institutional mechanism**
- **Source state**

## Example research targets

- women as teachers, transmitters, patrons, and students;
- hospitals and medical teaching;
- waqf-supported public goods;
- scholarly pluralism;
- treatment of animals;
- literacy, libraries, and translation;
- non-Muslim communities under different Muslim polities;
- legal and institutional variation across centuries.

### Guardrail

The Golden Ages are evidence of possible civilizational manifestations—not a second revelation and not a spotless apologetic age.

# 8. P1 — Trust, Privacy, and Accessibility

## Fonts

The current document loads Google Fonts while claiming no third-party code / tracking.

Preferred fix:

- self-host fonts;
- retain visual typography;
- remove Google network dependency.

Alternative:

- change footer wording to accurately disclose remote font delivery.

## Feedback storage

Clarify exactly what happens when a user submits a counter.

The page currently implies both private storage and no database.

Use explicit language such as:

> “Submitting opens a prefilled email. The site itself stores nothing.”

or disclose the actual storage mechanism.

## Correction address

Use a project-domain identity rather than an unrelated Gmail address when available.

## Astrolabe accessibility

Make every layer:

- keyboard focusable;
- activatable with Enter / Space;
- visibly focused;
- represented in a text alternative;
- usable without hover;
- readable with reduced motion.

## Mobile acceptance criterion

At 390 × 844 CSS pixels:

- no horizontal overflow;
- top method remains visible;
- Live cards are readable without zoom;
- copy actions require no precision tapping;
- target answer is reachable in under five seconds.

# 10. Acceptance Tests

## Source-layer comprehension

After a two-minute use test, a new user can accurately explain the difference between:

- Qur’an;
- Sunnah;
- hadith report;
- fiqh;
- state law;
- individual action.

## Hostile screenshot test

No isolated line should plausibly imply:

- public dishonesty;
- Qur’an-only sectarianism;
- wholesale dismissal of hadith;
- collective suspicion of another demographic;
- denial of documented wrongdoing;
- certainty beyond the evidence.

## Opponent steelman test

A knowledgeable Sunni traditionalist, secular historian, Christian critic, and hostile media editor should each fail to find an obvious factual or categorical error in the opening source constitution.

## Live retrieval test

A user can retrieve the correct response to six benchmark accusations in under five seconds each on mobile.

## Truth-boundary test

Every sensitive claim includes at least one explicit statement the advocate must not say.

## Provenance test

Every externally deployable factual claim has:

- a source;
- a confidence state;
- a correction path;
- a named owner or research queue when incomplete.

# 12. Claude Code Decision Rule

This specification is a **proposal**, not permission to blindly overwrite superior existing work.

Claude Code should:

- inspect the current implementation;
- preserve stronger existing components;
- flag conflicts with other repo doctrine;
- implement the smallest coherent slice first;
- document rejected suggestions and why;
- retain old versions as eval baselines;
- never silently convert provisional historical claims into public certainty.

> **The upgrade succeeds when the desk becomes faster in conflict, harder to misquote, more faithful to the source hierarchy, and more honest about uncertainty—without losing its force.**
