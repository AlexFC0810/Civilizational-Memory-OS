# Canonical Integration Gate

**Purpose:** Prevent raw agent output, persuasive prose, or large source dumps from entering canon without adversarial review.

> Canon is not where an idea first appears. Canon is where an idea arrives after surviving source audit, comparison audit, and breaker evidence.

## Gate 0 — Scope integrity

- Is the packet answering one independently testable question?
- Did the agent stay inside the comparison and source boundaries?
- Did the research question silently mutate?

**Fail condition:** split and resubmit.

## Gate 1 — Provenance

- Are primary sources identified?
- Are quotations traceable to editions, manuscripts, datasets, or stable publications?
- Are dates, translations, and textual variants visible?
- Are screenshots, Wikipedia, public summaries, and AI outputs used only as discovery aids?

**Fail condition:** no canon-level use.

## Gate 2 — Source-layer integrity

- Are scripture, oral report, biography, interpretation, law, institution, empire, custom, and modern polemic separated?
- Does each conclusion name the layer it actually establishes?

**Fail condition:** return for category repair.

## Gate 3 — Definition lock

- Are loaded, legal, clinical, and historical terms explicitly defined?
- Does the evidence satisfy each required element of the definition?
- Is an anachronistic term being used only with explanation?

**Fail condition:** label prohibited.

## Gate 4 — Comparative symmetry

- Is ideal compared with ideal?
- Is practice compared with practice?
- Are periods and material constraints reasonably matched?
- Is the historical baseline visible?
- Is one worldview receiving charitable interpretation while another receives hostile compression?

**Fail condition:** comparison invalid.

## Gate 5 — Adversarial completeness

- Is the strongest opposing case represented accurately?
- Has a red-team agent searched for breaker evidence?
- Are major counterexamples included rather than buried?
- Would a knowledgeable critic recognize their argument?

**Fail condition:** verdict remains provisional.

## Gate 6 — Causal integrity

- Are mediators, confounders, reverse causation, selection effects, and measurement problems mapped?
- Is correlation being narrated as causation?
- Are hidden or exported harms considered?

**Fail condition:** causal language narrowed.

## Gate 7 — Confidence calibration

Allowed statuses:

- established;
- probable;
- contested;
- unsupported;
- category error;
- unresolved.

Requirements:

- confidence score;
- explanation of uncertainty;
- what remains unknown;
- breaker evidence;
- review date.

**Fail condition:** no final-sounding public compression.

## Gate 8 — Claim minimization

Ask:

- What is the strongest sentence the evidence supports?
- What tempting stronger sentence does it not support?
- Can the claim be made narrower and more durable?

Examples:

- prefer `extraordinary early textual stability with bounded variants` over `not one letter ever varied`;
- prefer `serious source-critical challenge to the age report` over `completely debunked`;
- prefer `abolitionary vector/manumission architecture` over `Islam abolished slavery`;
- prefer `military-political founder and commander` over accepting or reversing a loaded `warlord` label.

## Gate 9 — Integration impact

Before acceptance, list every artifact affected:

- claim card;
- source ledger;
- framework;
- benchmark;
- public narrative;
- prompt;
- dataset;
- open issue;
- superseded verdict.

Contradictory canon must be updated or explicitly versioned. Do not allow silent inconsistency.

## Gate 10 — Publication separation

The system must preserve three layers:

1. **Raw research** — evidence and notes.
2. **Provisional synthesis** — reasoned verdict with uncertainty.
3. **Public compression** — concise wording that cannot exceed the provisional synthesis.

Public impact does not justify stronger certainty.

## Acceptance record

Every integrated packet should record:

- packet ID;
- scout agent;
- red-team agent;
- integration editor;
- accepted verdict;
- confidence;
- breaker evidence;
- files changed;
- date;
- next review trigger.

## Canonical principles

> The system must be harder to manipulate than the humans and agents contributing to it.

> A source dump is not synthesis. A persuasive synthesis is not truth closure. Truth closure is a versioned verdict whose evidence, uncertainty, comparison class, and falsifier remain visible.

> The repository wins when better evidence can defeat its favorite claim without defeating the repository.
