# Legendary Discovery Prompt Genome

**Status:** Canonical prompt library  
**Purpose:** Preserve the questions, prompt structures, and conversational moves that repeatedly generated breakthroughs across the Civilizational Memory OS, so the process can be reproduced rather than depending on accidental chemistry.

## Core insight

The breakthroughs in this project did not come from one perfectly specified prompt.

They emerged from a repeatable combination:

> **High-information seed + reference-universe stack + open runway + truth rails + recursive challenge + durable canonization**

The prompt creates permission and direction. The conversation supplies friction, correction, memory, and accumulated synthesis.

> **The magic is not maximal instruction. It is the right constraints around a sufficiently intelligent search.**

## The four-part prompt architecture

### 1. Seed

Provide the observation, transcript, discomfort, contradiction, or emerging intuition.

A good seed does not need to contain the full theory. It needs to contain the live anomaly.

Example:

> “I think the treatment of animals may be a better civilizational benchmark than wealth or technology.”

### 2. Reference-universe stack

Invoke lenses that contain rich implicit methods.

Examples:

- ASI;
- Sherlock Holmes;
- comparative metahistorian;
- Stockfish or Go strategist;
- first-principles architect;
- coral-belt Pareto;
- institution builder;
- memetic engineer;
- source critic;
- moral philosopher.

Reference universes should open useful search spaces, not become theatrical branding.

### 3. Open runway

Avoid overconstraining the response before the terrain has been explored.

Examples:

- “You have the wheel.”
- “What are you vying to share?”
- “Where does your mind go?”
- “Anything else that is dying to emerge?”

### 4. Truth rails

Prevent creative aperture from becoming confident invention.

Examples:

- distinguish evidence from inference;
- represent the strongest opposing case;
- preserve valid criticism;
- identify what would change the conclusion;
- do not weaken real achievements merely because they were not universal;
- compare contemporaries before applying modern hindsight;
- save durable insights and open dependencies to GitHub.

## Master discovery prompt

> I am giving you a seed rather than a finished specification. Use the strongest relevant lenses—ASI-grade synthesis, first principles, Sherlock-style anomaly detection, comparative metahistory, Coral-Belt Pareto, narrative intelligence, and institution design. Identify the deepest breakthrough beneath the surface topic, connect it to adjacent systems, state the strongest current conclusion, and distinguish evidence, inference, hypothesis, and imaginative extension. Represent the strongest rival account, identify what would change your mind, and refuse both bureaucratic flattening and unsupported certainty. Find the golden threads, memorable formulations, transferable questions, public narratives, and institutions that should follow. Then recursively inspect the process itself: what did this conversation teach us about how to think, prompt, research, and build? Preserve everything durable in the AIOS, rank the next actions, and leave unresolved work visibly unresolved. You have the wheel.

## Minimal high-aperture prompts

These prompts are powerful because they express trust while keeping the search directional.

- **What are you vying to share?**
- **You have the wheel as ASI.**
- **Where are you vying to go with this?**
- **What is the deepest thing here that I have not asked?**
- **What should exist because this insight exists?**
- **What would a maximally integrated intelligence notice?**
- **What is the hidden breakthrough beneath the apparent topic?**
- **What does this connect to across history, media, psychology, and institutions?**

## Golden-thread prompts

Use these after several ideas have accumulated.

- What is the golden thread connecting everything we just uncovered?
- Which idea is upstream of the others?
- Which discovery changes the meaning of the entire conversation?
- What should this breakthrough be called so it becomes reusable?
- What new primitive has emerged?
- Which ideas are separate, and which are manifestations of the same deeper mechanism?
- What is the one sentence that makes the rest easier to understand?
- What would be lost if this thread were summarized conventionally?
- What should become canon versus research backlog versus public narrative?

## Opinionated-synthesis prompts

Use these to prevent the model from hiding behind neutral enumeration.

- What is your strongest current synthesis?
- What do you think most likely happened, and why?
- Which explanation has the highest causal coverage with the fewest unsupported assumptions?
- What is the narrative of this period, civilization, or controversy?
- What mattered most rather than merely what occurred?
- Which interpretation is closest to truth after integrating the strongest evidence from all sides?
- Where is false balance preventing clarity?
- What conclusion is the evidence already strong enough to state directly?
- What would you say if you had to advise a civilization rather than write a literature review?

## Source and truth-discipline prompts

- Which exact authority layer made this claim?
- What true fragment makes the larger narrative persuasive?
- What causal bridge is being assumed rather than demonstrated?
- Are we comparing ideal with ideal and practice with practice?
- Compared with whom, in which period, under which material constraints?
- Which claim is event truth, corpus truth, reception truth, normative truth, or institutional truth?
- Which caveats are load-bearing, and which merely anesthetize the sentence?
- What evidence would break the current conclusion?
- What part of our preferred account is most vulnerable?
- Where are critics held to a lower standard than defenders—or vice versa?
- What achievement is being erased because it was not universal?
- What harm is being excused as “failed humans” despite recurring architecture?

## Coral-Belt Pareto prompts

- What is the smallest loop that improves the most layers simultaneously?
- What one move makes several later moves easier or unnecessary?
- Where is the actual bottleneck rather than the most visible task?
- Which project creates reusable research, narrative, distribution, learning, and institutional option value?
- What should be killed, merged, deferred, or subordinated?
- Where is documentation creating more coordination cost than capability?
- Which action has the highest truth impact × reach × reuse × transfer × compounding, adjusted for distortion and fragility?
- What is P0, P1, and merely interesting?
- What should remain manual until repeated use proves the software requirement?
- What is the coral-belt move here—not merely the 80/20 move?

## Recursive self-improvement prompts

- What did this conversation teach us about how the AI failed?
- Which correction should become a future default?
- Was the failure caused by missing context, excessive caution, weak synthesis, poor compression, issue sprawl, or lack of execution?
- What valid work should be preserved while replacing the failed output?
- Which adjacent artifacts inherit the same flaw?
- What protocol or prompt should change?
- What new failure mode might the correction introduce?
- How will we test that the improvement is real rather than rhetorically satisfying?
- What should the AIOS remember so this mistake becomes less likely everywhere?

## Canonization prompts

- What from this conversation is canon, working synthesis, frontier hypothesis, public narrative, research packet, or issue?
- Which insight exists only in chat and therefore remains institutionally nonexistent?
- Can a future agent reconstruct the meaning without this conversation?
- Are the sources, uncertainty, corrections, and next actions preserved?
- Which files need updating rather than creating another parallel artifact?
- What should be indexed in `START_HERE.md`?
- What has been superseded?
- Which issue is the executable trunk?
- What remains genuinely unresolved?
- Is the thread closed as conversation while remaining open as research and production?

## The Legendary Session Protocol prompt

> Treat this conversation as a live research laboratory, not a disposable chat. Begin from my seed, but search for the deeper mechanism, neglected comparison, hidden moral question, and institution that may be waiting underneath it. Use reference universes as methods rather than costumes. Be opinionated where evidence permits, explicit where inference begins, and adversarial toward both our critics and our preferred story. Do not let caution erase real achievements, and do not let narrative force outrun truth. Surface the golden threads, memorable language, reusable question genes, creator opportunities, and compounding loops. When I correct you, diagnose the default that failed and update the system rather than merely apologizing. At natural milestones, convert discoveries into canon, protocols, prompts, research packets, public assets, and ranked execution. End with a repository state from which a later agent can continue without access to the chat.

## Why the prompt works

The architecture avoids two common failures.

### Over-specification

A giant checklist can force the model to execute the user's existing map rather than discover a better one.

### Empty openness

“You have the wheel” without context, truth rails, memory, or a live anomaly can produce eloquent drift.

The high-performance zone is:

> **Enough structure to preserve truth and mission; enough aperture for emergence.**

## Prompt evaluation dimensions

Prompts should be tested on:

- depth;
- novelty;
- causal coherence;
- evidence discipline;
- adversarial survivability;
- moral clarity;
- public memorability;
- transferability;
- canon readiness;
- execution usefulness;
- user resonance;
- recursive learning produced.

## Canonical close

> **The best prompt does not dictate the answer. It creates the conditions in which a better question, synthesis, and institution can emerge.**

> **Open runway without truth rails becomes drift. Truth rails without open runway become bureaucracy. The legendary prompt architecture requires both.**
