## 3. Canonical adversary model

The project does not fight populations.

It fights false permissions:

- weakness as permission;
- ownership as sovereignty;
- secrecy as erasure;
- legality as morality;
- tradition as immunity;
- religious labeling as sanctification;
- modernity as innocence;
- distance as nonparticipation;
- spectatorship as action;
- cruelty as strength;
- group approval as absolution;
- out-group cruelty as self-exemption.

## 5. Production algorithm

Every asset should:

1. identify one false permission;
2. reveal one truthful reversal;
3. offer one guardian identity;
4. provide one safe action;
5. use one primary mode;
6. preserve source distinctions;
7. avoid graphic spectacle;
8. pass the scorecard;
9. receive native review when localized;
10. connect attention to a protection outcome.

The four memetic functions are:

> **Arrest → Reveal → Recode → Convert**

## 6. Required modes

### Universal

Begin from moral recognition without theological prerequisite.

### Explicit Islamic

Show Qur’anic and Prophetic architecture openly and accurately.

### Muslim accountability

Apply the Islamic standard inwardly to Muslim conduct and institutions.

### Comparative mercy

Represent traditions strongly and preserve real differences.

### Child protective action

Provide safe, rehearsed steps without asking children to confront dangerous offenders alone.

Do not force one asset to perform all modes simultaneously.

## 7. Automatic rejection conditions

Reject content that:

- materially lies;
- converts uncertainty into certainty;
- dehumanizes a population;
- uses graphic cruelty for engagement;
- exploits tragedy as conversion pressure;
- implies every halal slaughter is painless;
- collapses Qur’an, hadith, fiqh, history, and current Muslim practice;
- invites harassment of minors;
- launches unreviewed translations;
- lacks a safe child action pathway;
- values Islamic reputation above victim protection;
- optimizes only for reach.

## 9. Definition of success

A meme is not complete because it is repeated.

It is complete when repetition:

- preserves truth;
- dignifies the vulnerable;
- changes perception;
- creates guardian identity;
- produces safe action;
- converts into institution;
- remains correctable;
- protects future life.

> **Verified protection events per unit of attention.**
