# Islamic Smear Defense Program

**Status:** Canonical research and deployment agenda v1.0  
**Created:** 2026-07-14  
**Governing doctrine:** `canon/TRUTH_CONSTRAINED_ZEALOUS_ADVOCACY_DOCTRINE.md`

## Objective

Build the strongest truth-constrained defense library for high-impact accusations against Islam, the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, Muslims, and Islamic civilization.

The program is not an indiscriminate apologetics engine. It distinguishes:

- false smear;
- distorted half-truth;
- contested historical claim;
- valid criticism attached to an invalid conclusion;
- genuine injustice committed under an Islamic label;
- blueprint, interpretation, institution, state, culture, movement, and individual conduct.

The goal is not to make every accusation disappear. The goal is to produce the correct verdict, enforce the correct burden, and prevent dishonest framing from defeating truth through asymmetry.

## Program architecture

Every dossier must contain:

1. **Exact charge** — the accusation stated in falsifiable propositions.
2. **Hidden premises** — every unspoken bridge required for the conclusion.
3. **Source-layer map** — Qur’an, hadith, tafsir, fiqh, state, empire, culture, movement, individual.
4. **Burden map** — which party must establish which proposition.
5. **Strongest prosecution case** — internal red-team version.
6. **Evidence audit** — provenance, date, transmission, independence, context, contradiction, selection effects.
7. **Strongest defense** — affirmative and exculpatory case.
8. **Inference audit** — what does and does not follow.
9. **Cross-examination tree** — questions that force specificity.
10. **Compression ladder** — 15 seconds, 30–60 seconds, complete treatment.
11. **Clip-proof line** — survives hostile editing.
12. **Reversal** — selective standard, contradiction, or burden failure.
13. **Truth boundary** — prohibited overclaims and unresolved questions.
14. **Source status** — open, provisional, source-closed, specialist-reviewed.
15. **Deployment context** — social reply, long-form article, debate, creator live mode, scholarly exchange.

These dominate public perception and should be source-closed first.

#### S2. “Islam spread by the sword”

#### S3. Jihad / “Islam commands permanent war and terrorism”

#### A7. “No compulsion” versus coercive history

#### A8. Science and knowledge / “Islam caused stagnation”

## Highest-leverage operating insight

Smears win by compression asymmetry:

> **The accusation is vivid, morally loaded, and ten seconds long. The defense is hesitant, technical, and five minutes long.**

The program must reverse that asymmetry without sacrificing truth.

Every completed dossier must therefore produce a short answer at least as memorable as the smear, backed by a much deeper evidence substrate.

## Canonical maxim

> **Do not merely answer the accusation. Put the accusation itself on trial.**
