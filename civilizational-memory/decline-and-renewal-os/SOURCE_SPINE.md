## Claim Card Workflow

Every public-facing claim should include:

1. Claim.
2. Source.
3. Evidence grade.
4. Caveat.
5. Safe wording.
6. Unsafe wording.
7. Content angle.
8. Visual angle.

## Evidence Grades

- **A:** strong mainstream support.
- **B:** strong but needs nuance.
- **C:** strategic synthesis; needs source work.
- **D:** do not publish yet.
