# Abrahamic Criticism Source Ledger

**Status:** Research backlog / source verification ledger  
**Use case:** Track claims that need primary-source verification before public-facing arguments are built.  
**Rule:** Do not promote a claim to “load-bearing” until source, context, confidence, and counterarguments are logged.

---

## 1. Purpose

This ledger exists because Civilizational Memory OS must not become a pile of persuasive but under-verified claims.

The goal is:

> Strong claims, honestly sourced, with confidence levels and unresolved tensions clearly marked.

## 5. Source promotion workflow

For each claim:

1. Locate primary source.
2. Verify exact wording.
3. Verify date/context.
4. Identify interpretive range.
5. Identify strongest counterargument.
6. Assign confidence.
7. Write public-safe version.
8. Link back to framework/matrix entry.

---

## 6. Public-facing claim rules

Before using a claim publicly:

- Use exact wording where possible.
- Avoid “first,” “always,” “never,” “proved,” and “debunked” unless warranted.
- Distinguish primary source from later tradition.
- Mark uncertainty clearly.
- Name unresolved tensions.
- Avoid defending corruption to protect the seed.
- Avoid attacking the seed because of corruption.

---

## 7. Compressed synthesis

> The source ledger keeps Civilizational Memory OS honest. No claim becomes load-bearing until its source layer, historical context, confidence level, and counterarguments are visible.
