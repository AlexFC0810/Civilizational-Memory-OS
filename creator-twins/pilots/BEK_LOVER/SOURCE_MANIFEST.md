## Priority rules

### P0 — Foundational

Sources that appear to contain:

- the strongest broad historical synthesis;
- recurring worldview statements;
- explicit source recommendations;
- intellectual autobiographical context;
- major reversals or corrections;
- high-value upcoming use cases.

### P1 — Domain depth

Sources developing one major topic, era, person, or causal model in depth.

### P2 — Evolution and contradiction

Sources useful for dating changes, tensions, or apparent inconsistencies.

### P3 — Supporting archive

Short appearances, clips, posts, or repetitive material that may add examples, language, or audience context.

## Corpus selection rule

Do not ingest purely by recency or popularity.

Optimize for:

> **worldview coverage × originality × source density × causal depth × evolution value × creator usefulness**
