## 2. Create the Jesus Fruit Lens packet

### Core question

> **If Islam were a demonic seed, what fruit should we predict—and how well does that prediction explain fourteen centuries of actual civilizational output?**

### Evidence clusters

- uncompromising monotheism;
- repeated refuge from Satan;
- prayer and fasting;
- obligatory charity;
- care for travelers, debtors, orphans, and animals;
- hospitals and medical institutions;
- translation and advancement of knowledge;
- waqf infrastructure;
- Jewish cultural flourishing in important periods;
- Albanian rescue of Jews;
- internal traditions condemning tyranny and cruelty.

### Guardrail

Fruit cannot by itself prove revelation. Bad systems can produce some good outcomes, and good systems can be corrupted. The lens tests totalizing demonization, then routes to direct textual and historical inquiry.

### Legendary line

> **Before calling the tree demonic, account for the hospitals, libraries, endowments, prayers, rescued minorities, and moral concern for a thirsty dog growing from its branches.**

## 6. Turn collective blame into institutional graphing

Whenever a transcript says:

- “they own it”;
- “they control it”;
- “they are planning it”;
- “the elites”;
- an ethnic/religious group as agent;

run the **Actor Resolution Compiler**:

1. Which specific person?
2. In which formal role?
3. Which institution?
4. What ownership or authority?
5. Which decision?
6. Which contract, funding flow, or policy?
7. Which measurable output?
8. Which alternative explanation?
9. Which evidence of coordination?
10. Which evidence would falsify the claim?

Result:

> Criticism becomes more dangerous to actual misconduct and less dangerous to innocent populations.
