## Segment-level interpretation

This exposes a category rule without requiring agreement on theology.

### BL-SEG-002 — Information as moral liability

This is a potential **creator covenant** for the entire Supercharger Network.

The Supercharger must preserve the history while removing collective blame and unfalsifiable ownership narratives.
