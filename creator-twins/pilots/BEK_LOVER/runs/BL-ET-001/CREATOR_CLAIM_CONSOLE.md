# BL-ET-001 — Creator Claim Console

**Status:** Manual MVP  
**Purpose:** Convert the transcript audit into an operational preparation instrument for creator validation, future appearances, public assets, and correction.

## Operating rule

Every consequential claim must remain visibly separated into six states:

1. **Said** — what the creator actually expressed.
2. **Reconstructed** — the strongest fair interpretation.
3. **Verified** — supported at the stated scope.
4. **Needs narrowing** — true fragment, excessive wording.
5. **Speculation** — hypothesis, theology, prediction, or incomplete inference.
6. **Corrected** — superseded wording with a stronger replacement.

No claim moves into a public creator packet without:

- source provenance;
- speaker attribution;
- scope;
- audience route;
- public wording;
- creator approval where representation is involved.
